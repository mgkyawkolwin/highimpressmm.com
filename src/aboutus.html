About Us

Well-established company with proven backgrounds

Our first step towards our success was founded in 2015. The company was originally known as an established firm and had achieved several milestones. We had many success and we had a growth of human resources in a very short time with over 32 employees.

High Impress Company Limited was established in 2016 together with the strong members of board of directors as a group of companies.
The group comprises the following sub-divisions

  • Construction
  • Import & Export
  • Wholesale textile
  • Fitness center
  • Fashion shop
  • Hotel Supplement

Our main strength and interests are in construction and Import & Export business. We have already done several construction projects, having total contract value of over several billion kyats, in all public and private sectors with success. We are also importing and supplying heavy machineries, chemical products, clothing, equipments, riot control & firm arm equipments to ministries, governmental organizations and non-govermental organizations.
