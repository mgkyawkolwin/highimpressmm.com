No.2 Police Training School

This governmental building was constructed for Ministry of Home Affair. This project is located at Wat Htee Kan City of Bago division. The construction was started on November, 2017 and completed successfully on May, 2018. The total project value is well over 400 million kyats. The type of the construction is two-storeys RCC building. The project also includes separate dining house and 10 bathrooms.
