2.5 Stories Residential Building

This huge residential building was constructed in Chan Aye Thar Zan township of Mandalay city. The construction was started on January, 2018 and completed in December, 2018. This is two-and-half-storeys RCC building and the contract value is nearly 80 million kyats. This building area was over 3600 square feet. The project included water and electricity installation as well.
